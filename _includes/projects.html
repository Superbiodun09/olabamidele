Crime Statistics - Ministry of Police Affairs

  • Power Qwery | Power Pivot | Power Bi

This data analyzes crime statistics in Nigeria from 2021 to 2023, revealing significant trends and regional disparities. Total reported cases varied, with 3,965 in 2021, 6,359 in 2022, and 5,647 in 2023. High-crime states include Kaduna, Zamfara, and Edo, necessitating targeted interventions. Kidnapping and murder are the most prevalent crimes, with notable occurrences of armed robbery, banditry, and terrorism. The peak in 2022 suggests a temporary escalation in criminal activities. These insights underscore the need for tailored security strategies to address specific regional challenges and improve public safety across Nigeria.
